Tulsa Automation LLP offers cutting-edge security services including intruder detection, facial recognition , and other advanced surveillance solutions designed to protect your property and assets. Our AI-driven intruder detection systems use motion sensors, smart analytics, and real-time alerts to identify unauthorized access and provide immediate notifications, ensuring quick responses. The facial recognition technology enhances security by automatically identifying and verifying individuals at entry points, enabling contactless access control and improving overall safety. Our solutions are built to integrate seamlessly with existing security infrastructures, offering scalable, reliable, and real-time monitoring for both commercial and residential properties. With advanced analytics and intelligent automation, Tulsa Automation LLP ensures a proactive, 24/7 security solution to safeguard your premises.

TULSA Automation LLP enhances women's safety through its AI-based security solutions, including push-button alerts, smart surveillance, and real-time monitoring. Here’s how these technologies work:

1. Emergency Push Buttons (Panic Buttons)

  • Installed in public areas, workplaces, and residential buildings.
  • A single press sends an immediate distress signal to security teams or emergency responders.
  • Can be integrated with CCTV to capture live footage when pressed.

2. AI-Powered CCTV & Facial Recognition

  • Smart cameras with AI can detect unusual movements, aggression, or distress signals.
  • Facial recognition helps track known offenders and alert authorities.
  • Auto-tracking ensures continuous monitoring of a person in distress.

3. Mobile App & Remote Alerts

  • A mobile app linked to security systems allows women to send SOS alerts.
  • Push notifications inform nearby security personnel for quick action.

4. Smart Street Lighting & Geo-Fencing

  • Automated lights brighten up when movement is detected in dark areas.
  • Geo-fencing alerts security teams when someone enters restricted zones.

5. Integrated Public Announcement System

  • Can be triggered with push-button alerts to warn or deter potential threats.
  • Security personnel can communicate with the affected individual instantly.
