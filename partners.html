SUKHDEV
Director

Sukhdev Singh, the driving force behind Tulsa Automation, brings over three decades of invaluable engineering experience to the company. Founded in 2023, Tulsa Automation benefits from Mr. Singh's extensive background in providing engineering services to manufacturing plants both in India and internationally. His career spans numerous projects, including significant contributions to the sugar industry in Kenya, giving him a unique perspective on the challenges and opportunities within diverse industrial settings. This global exposure has honed his ability to understand and address the specific needs of clients across different sectors. Mr. Singh's deep understanding of manufacturing processes, coupled with his commitment to innovation, forms the cornerstone of Tulsa Automation's mission. He is passionate about leveraging cutting-edge technologies to optimize operations, enhance efficiency, and deliver tailored automation solutions that empower businesses to thrive in today's competitive landscape. His leadership ensures that Tulsa Automation provides clients with not only technical expertise but also a strategic partner dedicated to their long-term success. This rich history in industrial engineering positions Tulsa Automation as a trusted provider of reliable and effective automation solutions.

MANJOT
CEO

Manjot is a seasoned security and surveillance professional with over 14 years of experience, offering a comprehensive suite of services encompassing business development, project management, and expert security consulting. His background includes successful tenures at industry leaders like Honeywell and Hikvision, where he consistently drove sales, led complex projects, and crafted tailored solutions for clients across Punjab and Jammu & Kashmir. Currently, as a distribution partner for Polestar CCTV and Uniview, Manjot leverages this extensive expertise to provide cutting-edge, reliable security systems to meet the diverse needs of businesses and individuals in Chandigarh, ensuring access to the latest technologies and unparalleled quality. His academic credentials include an MBA in International Business from Panjab University and a BE in Electrical, Electronics, and Communications Engineering from Chandigarh Engineering College.
