Advanced System

Tulsa Automation LLP is transforming the landscape of security and fire protection with its state-of-the-art AI-based systems. Specializing in early fire detection through smart sensor cables, real-time temperature monitoring, and intelligent CCTV surveillance, their solutions provide next-level protection. The sensor cables are equipped with AI algorithms that detect heat patterns and potential fire risks long before they become visible threats, sending out instant alerts to prevent accidents. By using AI-powered analytics, their systems continuously monitor environmental conditions, ensuring swift identification of any anomalies and enabling quick response times. The advanced CCTV solutions use motion detection, facial recognition, and behavior analytics to monitor and secure premises, providing enhanced security with automated threat detection. Tulsa Automation’s systems are designed for easy integration with existing security and fire safety frameworks, making them suitable for a range of industries—from manufacturing plants to commercial spaces. With cloud connectivity, users can access and manage the system remotely, offering complete control and real-time updates at their fingertips. By blending AI intelligence, automation, and seamless integration, Tulsa Automation LLP is redefining what’s possible in the world of fire safety, surveillance, and environmental monitoring, ensuring superior safety and operational efficiency  for businesses.
